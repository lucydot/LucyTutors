How do I tutor?

My tutoring philosophy is “build mathematical confidence and the rest will follow”. You can use the internet to learn step-by-step mathematical recipes, but a good tutor will build confidence and make sure that your conceptual understanding is solid.

  • I tutor in a friendly, positive way to build mathematical confidence
  • I use whiteboards, diagrams and objects (counters, blocks) to aid conceptual understanding
  • I work with the student to find the misunderstandings that lead to mathematical mishaps
